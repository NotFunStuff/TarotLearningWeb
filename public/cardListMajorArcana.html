Major Arcana

Meaning of All Major Arcanas cards

The Major Arcana Tarot cards represent life lessons and major themes that are affecting life. The meaning of the Major Arcana card is very deep and complex and has many different interpretations (depending on the situation).

Also known as Trump Cards, the Major Arcana cards consist of 21 numbered cards and 1 unnumbered card (The Fool). The Fool is the protagonist of the Major Arcana and makes his journey through each card, meeting new people and learning new life lessons along the way, and finally completing the journey with the card. The World. This is called the Fool's Journey and is a great way to understand the storyline of the Major Arcana Tarot card's meaning.

Meaning of one Major Arcanas card

When you see the Major Arcana in your Tarot reading, you are being prompted to reflect on the lessons and themes of life at this time. The Major Arcana will usually be important cards and affect the entire Tarot spread and the surrounding cards will complement the Major Arcana.

What if most of the cards spread are Major Arcana

When in a Tarot spread mostly Major Arcana cards, you are experiencing significant life-changing events that will have lasting effects. This is when you will have important lessons that you need to heed for a better future.
